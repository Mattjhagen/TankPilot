TankPilot Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Information We Collect

  • Location (GPS) while you're using navigation and fuel-related features, to estimate your position relative to your fuel range and nearby stations.
  • Bluetooth permissions for connecting to compatible OBD-II adapters.
  • Network access to retrieve fuel station information.
  • Crash information provided by Android, if enabled on your device.
  • TankPilot does not collect your contacts, messages, photos, or microphone data.

How Information Is Used

  • To calculate trips.
  • To power Fuel Rescue — finding reachable fuel stations.
  • To generate maintenance reminders.
  • To support Android Auto functionality.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

Location data is processed only to provide the app functionality you've requested — it is not shared with third parties for advertising or other unrelated purposes.

Security

We use industry-standard safeguards to help protect your information.

Your Rights

You may uninstall the app at any time, which stops all data collection by TankPilot on your device.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at support@tankpilot.app.
