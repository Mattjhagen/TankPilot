What is TankPilot?

TankPilot is an Android app that turns your phone into a fuel and driving companion. Using your phone's GPS, it estimates how much fuel you have left and how far you can safely go, then helps you find a reachable gas station before your tank runs low with Fuel Rescue.

Alongside fuel estimation, TankPilot tracks your trips and keeps you ahead of your vehicle's maintenance schedule, all through a clean, distraction-free dashboard designed to be read at a glance while driving. TankPilot also runs natively in the car through Android Auto support.

Bluetooth OBD-II support — for live diagnostics and richer vehicle data straight from your car's onboard computer — is currently under active development.
